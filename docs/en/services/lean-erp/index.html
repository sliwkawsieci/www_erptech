Lean ERP – Waste Elimination Methodology

At ERPtech, we implement ERP systems based on Lean Manufacturing principles. We eliminate waste, optimize flows, and build continuous improvement cultures – all within one integrated system.

Waste elimination. We identify and remove 8 types of Muda (overproduction, waiting, transport, overprocessing, inventory, unnecessary movement, defects, unused potential).

Continuous improvement (Kaizen). Built-in KPI monitoring mechanisms, bottleneck analysis, and optimization suggestions.

Value stream mapping. Mapping processes from raw material to finished product with clear visualization of value added.

Just-in-Time (JIT). Production and deliveries at the right time, minimizing buffer inventory, and pull system instead of push.

Five Lean ERP Principles

Fundamental Lean Manufacturing principles implemented in ERP systems ensure maximum operational efficiency and waste elimination.

1

Define Value

Determine what is valuable from the customer's perspective

2

Map Value Stream

Identify all steps and eliminate waste

3

Ensure Flow

Remove obstacles and ensure smooth value flow

4

Introduce Pull System

Produce only what the next step requires

5

Pursue Perfection

Kaizen – continuous improvement seeking
